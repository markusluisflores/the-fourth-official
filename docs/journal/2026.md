But the relevance gate is calibrated from **one** off-topic data point (cricket question at 0.309 vs threshold 0.35 — margin 0.041), and no on-topic `maxSimilarity` was ever recorded. Part 2a therefore adds gate metrics plus two new question tiers (should-abstain off-topic set; paraphrased/colloquial set not authored from chunk text) and recalibrates the threshold from a measured distribution, not an anecdote.
